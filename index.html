Trivia

  1. Beagles have an incredible sense of smell and tracking instinct.
  2. They were originally bred for hunting hare.
  3. Beagles are known for their friendly and gentle disposition.
  4. Snoopy from Peanuts is a famous cartoon Beagle.
  5. They are one of the most popular dog breeds in the U.S.
